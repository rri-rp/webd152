Our Story

Pacific Trails Resort began with a simple passion- kayaking. After moving from Southern California to the rugged Northern California coast, our founder found a deeper connection to nature through the ocean. What started as a personal escape became something more meaningful. Kayaking became a way to find peace, clarity, and community. Kayaking remains at the heart of our story, it’s more than an activity, it’s what inspired everything we do.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a relaxing and meaningful outdoor experience while preserving the natural beauty of the California coast. We believe nature should be experienced, not rushed. Adventure creates meaningful memories. Connection is built through shared experiences, and the ocean brings peace and perspective.
